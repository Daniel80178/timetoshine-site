Get Started

Copy the strategy in 4 steps

A clean walkthrough — open account, link funds, start copying. About 15 minutes from sign-up to your first auto-replicated trade.

1

Open a Pepperstone account

You need an account with our broker, Pepperstone, to access the strategy. Use the link below to get the right setup with our affiliate.

  • Click the link below — it opens Pepperstone's sign-up
  • Fill in your personal details and complete standard identity verification (driver's licence or passport)
  • When prompted to choose a platform, select cTrader
Open Pepperstone Account →
2

Create a cTrader USD sub-account

Once your broker profile is approved, log into your Pepperstone Client Area and open a new trading sub-account with these exact settings to match the strategy:

  • Platform: cTrader
  • Base currency: USD
  • Leverage: 1:500
  • Minimum deposit: $750 USD

Check your email — Pepperstone sends you a unique cTrader ID (cTID) and password after the sub-account is created. You'll need these in Step 3.

Pepperstone Client Area →
3

Log in to cTrader Copy

cTrader Copy is the social terminal where the strategy lives. Sign in there with the cTID credentials you got via email.

  • Open the TIMETOSHINE strategy page (link below)
  • Log in with your cTID and password
  • Click the Copy icon in the left vertical menu
Open TIMETOSHINE →
4

Link your funds and start copying

The final step — link your deposited money to the strategy. Trades will replicate to your account automatically from this point.

  • On the strategy page, click the green Start Copying button (top right)
  • Select your funded USD Pepperstone cTrader account from the dropdown
  • Allocate your $750+ USD investment amount
  • Review the fee structure, then click Start Copying at the bottom to confirm

From this moment, every trade we open replicates automatically into your account, scaled to your allocation.

Start Copying →
